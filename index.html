Madrassathul Fathah is an Islamic educational institution operating under the guidance of Al-Fathah Islamic Center, dedicated to providing students with a strong foundation in religious and moral education.

  • Preparing students with essential Islamic knowledge and life skills.

Why Choose Our Madrassa?

At Madrassathul Fathah, we are committed to providing a high-quality Islamic education within a nurturing and supportive environment. We take pride in our qualified teachers (Usthads), who are dedicated to guiding students in both religious studies and personal development. We also focus on tracking each student’s daily activities to ensure their academic and spiritual growth.

  • Peaceful and welcoming environment for focused learning.
  • Regular events to strengthen community and enhance learning.
  • Dedicated teachers guiding students through each step of their education.
  • Close monitoring of students' daily progress to support their development.
